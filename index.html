Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ZeroMiss ("the App") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects the following types of information:

  • Information Stored on Your Device: All data you enter into the App, including patient information, tasks, and notes, is stored exclusively in a secure area on your device. This information is never automatically transmitted to any external servers.
  • Information Transmitted for External Services:
    • Announcements Feature: When using the announcements feature, the App communicates with a server built on Google Apps Script (GAS), provided by Google. This communication is solely for the purpose of checking for new announcements. No personally identifiable information or patient data from your device is ever transmitted.

2. Use of Information

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • To Provide App Functionality: To provide features such as patient information management, task reminders, and calculation tools.
  • To Deliver Announcements: To deliver important notices and updates regarding the App.

3. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law.

4. Stopping Collection of Information

The App does not offer a feature to stop the collection of information. If you wish to stop the collection, please uninstall the App.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the contact information provided on the App Store product page.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time due to changes in laws or our services. The latest version of the Privacy Policy will be available on this page.
